About Connectgold

Connectgold is a next-generation, high-performance digital ad network designed to bridge the gap between global advertisers and independent publishers. Powered by edge-computed real-time bidding, we deliver sub-millisecond ad serving, transparent revenue distribution, and seamless monetization for modern websites and apps.

🚀 Our Mission

To empower publishers with fair, transparent earnings while providing advertisers with targeted, high-converting ad placements across verified web properties globally.

Why Connectgold?

Traditional ad networks often lock creators into opaque revenue models or slow down websites with heavy third-party code. Connectgold changes the game:

💰 80% Publisher Share

Multi-Format Ad Serving

Connectgold supports all major digital advertising formats, enabling publishers to optimize layouts for maximum engagement:

  • Banner Ads: Responsive leaderboards (728x90), medium rectangles (300x250), and mobile units (320x50) with customizable auto-rotation.
  • Native Content Units: Fully integrated native card ads that match your website's organic aesthetic.
  • Rewarded Video: High-eCPM video integrations with server-to-server reward callback notifications.
  • Interstitial Units: High-impact, full-screen transitions for maximum click-through performance.

Global Standards & Third-Party Integration

We believe in total flexibility. Connectgold provides automated ads.txt certification to prevent fraud and supports direct client-side event tracking into Google Analytics 4 (GA4), Plausible, and Mixpanel, allowing publishers to cross-verify every single impression in real time.
