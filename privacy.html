Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Skyl P World. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

When you log in using Google, we access basic profile information (like your name and email) to create your account. We also use LocalStorage to save your game favorites and session status locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to enhance your experience, track your "hearted" games, and provide support. We use Firebase and Google Analytics to monitor site performance and security.

3. Third-Party Services

We use GamePix to provide game content and EmailJS to handle support requests. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for advertising.

4. Security

Your session is protected by a 30-minute auto-logout feature to ensure your account remains secure even on shared devices.
