Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Welcome to SkyIPworld. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our AI Terminal and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We may collect non-sensitive identity markers (such as a Google User ID) strictly for managing your access to the platform.
  • Payment Status: We track your subscription or payment status to grant access to premium terminal features. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic diagnostic data, such as search impressions or interactions with our tools, to improve platform performance.

2. How We Store Your Data (Local Storage)

To provide a fast and secure experience, SkyIPworld utilizes your browser's localStorage. We store digital "keys" (like your payment status) directly on your device. This means your access data remains under your control and is not housed in a centralized database susceptible to breaches.

3. Third-Party Services & Integrations

Because SkyIPworld acts as an advanced terminal, we route specific requests through trusted third-party providers:

  • Payment Processing (Paystack): All payments (International, Local, American Express) are securely processed by Paystack. Your sensitive financial data is handled entirely by them and is never exposed to our servers.
  • AI and Environmental APIs: When you use tools like the Image Generator or Environmental Radar, your specific prompts or location queries are sent to our API partners to generate the result. We do not use your data to train these AI models.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with the necessary API processors mentioned above to facilitate the services you actively request within the terminal.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to clear your browser's local storage at any time, which will remove your local access keys. If you need assistance managing your account or payment status, please contact us.
