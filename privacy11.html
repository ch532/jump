Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026 | Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Connectgold Ad Network ("Connectgold", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy, security, and transparency of publishers, advertisers, and end-users who interact with our AI-driven, edge-computed ad serving infrastructure and online platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information across two distinct categories: platform account management and real-time ad auction execution.

A. Platform Account Data (Publishers & Advertisers)

  • Account Identifiers: Name, email address, and profile photo authenticated securely via Google Sign-In.
  • Financial & Payment Credentials: Transaction verification references (via Paystack API), bank routing details, and withdrawal configurations.
  • Tax & Compliance Declarations: Country of tax residency, Taxpayer Identification Numbers (TIN/SSN/NIN), entity classification, and W-8BEN/W-9 equivalent certifications required for global publisher payouts.

B. Ad Serving, Telemetry & AI Processing Data (End-Users)

When an ad delivered by Connectgold is rendered on a publisher's website or mobile WebView, our edge engine (broad-brook-39f4.trustconnect713.workers.dev) automatically processes telemetry to evaluate auction bids, prevent fraud, and render contextually matching native ads:

  • Network & Device Telemetry: Anonymized IP addresses (used exclusively for geo-targeting, frequency capping, and bot detection), browser user-agents, and viewport dimensions.
  • Coarse Location Data: Country and region headers processed at Cloudflare Edge nodes without storing pinpoint GPS coordinates.
  • Contextual Webpage Signals: Non-personal page titles and content tags processed via edge vector embeddings (Workers AI BGE model) to match relevant ads without cross-site tracking.
  • Interaction Metrics: Ad impression events, click timestamps, placement IDs, and rotation interval flags.

2. How We Use Collected Information

We use processed data strictly for operational, security, and regulatory purposes:

  • To execute real-time ad auctions, run contextual AI matching algorithms, generate dynamic CTA buttons, and serve lightweight ad units in under 100 milliseconds.
  • To process advertiser campaign budget deposits and credit the automated 80% revenue split directly to publisher KV accounts.
  • To enforce rate-limiting, frequency capping (preventing duplicate ad exposure), and detect invalid click farm or automated bot fraud.
  • To ensure strict compliance with global and regional tax reporting standards.

3. Privacy Compliance (GDPR, CCPA & NDPR)

Connectgold adheres strictly to major global data protection frameworks, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR):

  • EEA / UK Regional Consent Enforcement: In the EEA and UK, Connectgold requires explicit consent parameters (consent=1 or consent=0). If consent is denied, ad delivery is automatically blocked.
  • Zero Behavioral Profiling: We do not track users across un-affiliated third-party websites or construct persistent consumer behavioral profiles. Edge caching via AI Gateway maintains strict 1-hour expiration limits (TTL).

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Infrastructure

We never sell, rent, or trade personal data to third-party data brokers. Information is processed exclusively by trusted infrastructure providers necessary for network operations:

  • Payment Rails: Paystack API for handling campaign deposits and automated local bank transfers.
  • Edge Compute & AI Gateway: Cloudflare Workers, Workers AI, Vectorize, and Workers KV for executing edge auctions, caching AI prompts, and managing account balances.
  • Client-Side Analytics (Optional): Publisher-enabled measurement tools (Google Analytics 4, Plausible, Mixpanel) receive non-sensitive impression events directly from the user's browser.

5. Security & Data Retention

All platform API communications are encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.3. API secret keys and verification handlers reside securely inside isolated Cloudflare Worker runtime environments. Account credentials remain active while your account is in good standing.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to inspect, correct, or request deletion of personal account data held on Connectgold. Account holders can update or delete profile details by reaching out to our compliance team.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please contact our privacy compliance team through our Contact Us page or email us directly at: support@chyke.online.
