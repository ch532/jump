Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Java ADK Assistant handles your information.

Your credentials are used only to authenticate with Google's AI services. No data is collected or shared except as required for agent operation.

Data Security

  • Authentication: API keys provided by the user are stored locally within the IDE's secure storage and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Transmission: All communication between the plugin and Google AI services is encrypted via standard HTTPS protocols.
  • Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties.
