Privacy Policy for Progold

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Welcome to Progold ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, Progold (the "App"), which is distributed via the HUAWEI AppGallery.

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

To provide you with a premium, context-aware experience, the App utilizes various device permissions and data points. This data is processed locally on your device or transmitted securely to our backend services and platform partners.

  • Location Data Collection: The App collects precise or approximate location data to power features such as interactive maps, local weather updates, and context-aware event notifications.
  • Activity Recognition Data: We access your device’s physical activity recognition features to provide smart, real-time awareness features (e.g., detecting state changes to optimize app behavior).
  • Calendar Data Access: With your explicit permission, the App accesses your device's calendar data. This is used solely to display upcoming events and calculate dynamic event reminders or cultural milestone alerts natively within the app interface.
  • Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Scanning: The App scans for nearby Wi-Fi networks and Bluetooth devices. This data is utilized to determine environment awareness and provide localized context features.
  • In-App Purchase Transaction Data: When you buy consumable items or features within the App, transaction data (such as product ID, transaction time, and payment status) is processed to validate your purchase and unlock your digital goods. We do not store your credit card or bank details; all financial transactions are handled securely by the storefront platform.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

To ensure optimal performance, analytics, and service delivery, the App integrates with Huawei Mobile Services (HMS). The following data points are securely processed by Huawei on our behalf:

  • Huawei Analytics Event Data: We collect anonymous user interaction statistics (such as page views, button clicks, and feature usage) through Huawei Analytics to understand how users interact with Progold and to improve overall app quality.
  • Crash Report Data (AGConnect Crash): If the App experiences an unexpected error or crash, diagnostic data (such as device model, operating system version, and stack traces) is sent to Huawei AppGallery Connect. This allows us to quickly identify and fix bugs.
  • Push Notification Tokens: We collect unique push notification tokens generated by the Huawei Push Kit to deliver real-time system alerts, updates, and user notifications directly to your device.
Data Processor Note: All data collected via HMS tools is securely processed by Huawei Mobile Services and is subject to Huawei's strict global privacy and data protection standards.

3. Data Retention and Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information. We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data.

  • Local Data: Data such as calendar events and activity recognition remain strictly on your local device.
  • Analytical and Crash Data: Retained anonymously by our processing partners to track historical app stability trends.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party brokers or advertisers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this webpage and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
