Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2026

At SkyIPworld, accessible from your deployment domain, we respect your privacy and are committed to processing data transparently. This policy outlines how our Geospatial Node Terminal interacts with user environment metrics.

1. Information We Process Automatically

To provide our specialized geospatial mapping services, our system dynamically reads the following operational inputs directly via your web browser environment:

  • Hardware Coordinate Array (GPS Geolocation): With your explicit permission, we access your device’s latitude and longitude vectors to center the interactive map widget and deliver local meteorological intelligence.
  • Network Node Status: We monitor your connectivity architecture state (Online/Offline status) to ensure reliable streaming performance of our background matrix loops.

2. Third-Party Services & Authentication

Our workspace securely integrates trusted global infrastructure partners to deliver live node assets:

  • Google Identity Services: We implement Google Authentication to verify secure access to the protected workspace terminal. We do not store your private Google profile data or account passwords on our infrastructure.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Used strictly for analytics to diagnose platform stability, interface execution bugs, and system loading velocities.
  • Yandex Maps Engine: Used to render operational physical maps based on the system's generated coordinate vectors.

3. Data Retention

SkyIPworld does not maintain backend databases of your historical personal movements or coordinates. Geolocation data is utilized locally within your active browser session to compute instant forecast structures and is cleared permanently upon closing the terminal node tab.

4. User Consent

By authenticating and utilizing this terminal, you hereby consent to our processing of real-time session coordinates for immediate mapping and independent meteorological visualization purposes.
